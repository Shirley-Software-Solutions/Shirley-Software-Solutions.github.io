Design & Development

Workflow with clients is essential for smooth collaboration and project success. Whether you’re starting fresh or improving an existing site, I’m here to make the journey seamless and enjoyable.

Initial Consultation:

  • Discuss the client's needs, goals, and vision for their website.
  • Provide an overview of your services and process.

    • Proposal and Planning:

  • Create a detailed proposal outlining the project scope, timeline, and cost.
  • Understand their target audience and any specific features they require. Include revision policies.
  • Share design concepts with the client to gain insight into brand and user experience.

    • Prototype and Design:

  • Develop a sitemap and wireframes to outline the website's structure and layout.
  • Collect images and other media for website.
  • Present a preview of design prototype for feedback and approval.

    • Development:

  • Implement design using CMS platform of choice or preferred tech stack.
  • Integrate necessary functionalities, such as e-commerce, booking systems, or contact forms.
  • Ensure the site is responsive and optimized for different devices.

    • Client Review:

  • Share the completed website with the client for review.
  • Address any feedback and make necessary revisions.
  • Keep the client informed throughout the process.

    • Testing and Launch:

  • Test the website for functionality, performance, and compatibility.
  • Ensure all links, forms, and features work as intended.
  • Once approved, publish the website and provide the client with access.

    • Handover and Training:

  • Transfer ownership of the website to the client or add them as a collaborator with specific permissions.
  • Provide training on how to manage and update the site using Wix's dashboard.
  • Offer ongoing support or maintenance services if needed.

    • Follow-Up:

  • Check in with the client after the launch to ensure everything is running smoothly.
  • Gather feedback and ask for testimonials or referrals.

    Plans & Services

    Hourly

    The total hours spent on a project can vary depending on factors. A static website with a few additional pages and domain registration will range from $30 to $75 per hour.

    Design and development: 10-20 hours (a crucial part of the process to ensure user interfaces that are both visually appealing and highly functional. Duration depends on the complexity of the pages and the required features)

    Testing and debugging: 5-10 hours (to ensure everything works as expected on different devices and browsers)

    Domain registration and setup: 1-2 hours (to connect to domain registrar)

    For basic sites you might expect to spend around 16-32 hours, which would translate to a cost $240 to $960.

    Project-Based

    Low-end: $200–$500 for basic design and functionality. Domain registration and setup.

    Mid-range: $500–$900 for more polished work, professional branding, minor customizations, fully responsive design, and interactive elements.

    High-end: $900–$1,500 for advanced features, CMS collections, SEO optimization, eCommerce websites, or anything more than 5 pages.

    Dynamic Web Apps: $1,000–$2,000 for dynamic and scalable web applications, API integration, and and additional services that address our clients' diverse business needs.
